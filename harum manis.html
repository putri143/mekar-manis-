My Story

Growing Harum Manis Mangoes with passion since 2022

I've been helping my father nurture his Harum Manis Orchard since the start of it all alongside my siblings. Mostly I'd handle the packing and posting of the Harum Manis Mangoes.

Today we succesfully achieve that same dedication and responsibility to a higher quality. Every Harum Manis mango is hand-picked to ensure you receive fruit that is in time for perfect ripeness and burst with natural sweetness.
